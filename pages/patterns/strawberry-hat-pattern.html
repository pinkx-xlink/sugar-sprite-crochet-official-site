Strawberry Ruffle Brim Hat Crochet Pattern

  • Row 1

    Ch 3 and slst to the first ch to create a ring.

  • Row 2

    Ch 2, dc 11 into the center of the ring, then slst to the first dc to join.

  • Row 3

    Ch 2, increase in every st using dc. Slst to the first dc to join.

  • Row 4

    Ch 2, dc into the same st, the inc into the next. *(Dc, then inc into the next.)* Repeat *()* until the end of the row.

  • Row 5

    Ch 2, dc into the same st, dc into the next st, the inc into the next. *(Dc into the next 2 st, then inc into the next.)* Repeat *()* until the end of the row.

  • Row 6

    Ch 2, dc into the same st, dc into the next 2 st, the inc into the next. *(Dc into the next 3 st, then inc into the next.)* Repeat *()* until the end of the row.

  • Row 7

    Ch 2, dc into the same st, dc into the next 3 st, the inc into the next. *(Dc into the next 4 st, then inc into the next.)* Repeat *()* until the end of the row.

  • Row 8

    Ch 2, dc ito the same st. Dc into each remaining st. Slst to the first dc to join.

  • Row 9

    Repeat row 8 x4.

  • Row 10

    Switch colors, then ch 2 and hdc into the same st. Hdc into each remaining st. Slst to the first hdc to join.

  • Row 12

    Ch 2, (hdc 5) into the same st. (Hdc 5) into each remaining st, then slst to the first hdc to join.

  • Row 13

    Ch 2, hdc into the same st. Hdc into each remaining st. Slst to the first hdc to join.

  • Row 14

    Repeat row 13. Then ch 1, tie off, and cut yarn.
