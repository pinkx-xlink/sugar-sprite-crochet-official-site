What is SSC?

Sugar Sprite Crochet is a woman-owned sustainable fashion and home decor business with the goal to make the clothes and trinkets we love more exciting, accessible, customizable, long-lasting, and eco-friendly. With fast-fashion around every corner, I'm on a mission to provide as many people as possible with customizable and intentional fashion options and clothing that will last a lifetime.

Why did we create Sugar Sprite Crochet?

I've always been passionate about creating and DIY-ing everything from my clothes to my decorations. My grandma taught me how to sew when I was nine years old after she found me trying to create my own plushie by tying pieces of scrap fabric together with yarn. Soon after, she granted me the gift of crochet! It was love at first stich.

In elementary school, I began to sell handmade "yarn dolls" for a whopping 25 cents each; in middle school, I shipped out my first custom handsewn dragon plushie. In high school, I branched out into knitting; I was overjealous at the beginning, so I figured I'd sell a few of the many hats I'd whipped up after school.

At age 16, I made my first crochet crop top, and received requests from my classmates to make some for them, too!

At 21, I had a vision of doing what I want for a living; at 23, I built my business: Sugar Sprite Crochet.

Now I'm 24. I've made my first few dozen sales, and I'm so excited to see what happens next!
